Lemon-Glazed Carrots

Description

Very easy and quick to make!

Ingrediants

  • 2 carrots, sliced 1/4-inch thick
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

Step 1

Place carrots into a large pot and cover with water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until carrots are tender, about 8 minutes. Drain./p>

Step 2

Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir carrots, brown sugar, and lemon juice in the melted butter, stirring often, until sugar has dissolved, 2 minutes.
